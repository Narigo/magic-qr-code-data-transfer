Magic QR Code data transfer

It works without a server!

How will it work?

To send data:

  1. You enter your data in a big text area
  2. It splits the text into multiple chunks that can be put into individual QR codes (QR codes can only be of length X-bytes)
  3. It creates individual QR codes based of the chunks, having something like MQR<index>.<length>.<data-of-chunk>

On the receiver:

  1. There is a reader to use the webcam and shows you which QR codes were scanned until you have all of them scanned completely
  2. It shows the whole data in a big text area at the receiver
